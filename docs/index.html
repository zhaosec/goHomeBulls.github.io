    Want to kill your time? Are your boring right now, waiting for class? Now today is your lucky day.

 

Participate UBlized Game!!

 

    Our game is called goHomeBulls. The goal of  game is to push the bulls to the specific destinations, and the destinations are barns shaped. You can control the little man by pressing the arrows from the keyboard. Be aware of the stamina, the game will be aborted if you run out of the stamina. We have game money called Dining Dollar.  It will be decreased by each steps you moved, but you can increase your stamina by shopping at the Stamina Shop by your dinning dollar. The Dining Dollar will be given based on the steps of little man has moved. Be aware of the trap in the game, if you fall into the trap the game will be aborted as well. Every steps will be counted from the little man's moving and you can record your scores to hall of fame. If you want more information click the video link above.

If you find any bugs, click "Bug report" above to let us know.
